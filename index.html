    Spawts
    CS3240: Interaction design Project

    Spawts is a platform for pet owners to find people to help them take care of their pets when they are away. Spawts gives owners a platform to post a listing of when they will be away and how much they are willing to pay for a pet sitter to take care of their pets. Pet sitters can then browse available listings and chat up the owner for details.
